# Glossary of Key Terms for Inclusive Education in Palestine

This glossary provides definitions of essential terms related to disability studies and inclusive education in both English and Arabic.

## Terms

### 1. Inclusive Education
- **English:** Inclusive Education
- **Arabic:** التعليم الدامج
- **Definition:** An educational approach that ensures all students, regardless of their abilities or disabilities, learn together in the same classroom with appropriate support and accommodations.

### 2. Disability
- **English:** Disability
- **Arabic:** إعاقة
- **Definition:** A condition that may limit a person's movements, senses, or activities, and may require adjustments to enable full participation in education and society.

### 3. Assistive Technology
- **English:** Assistive Technology
- **Arabic:** التكنولوجيا المساعدة
- **Definition:** Devices, software, or equipment that help individuals with disabilities perform functions that might otherwise be difficult or impossible.

### 4. Individualized Education Plan (IEP)
- **English:** Individualized Education Plan (IEP)
- **Arabic:** خطة التعليم الفردية
- **Definition:** A written document developed for a student with a disability that outlines specific educational goals, accommodations, and services tailored to the student's needs.

### 5. Accessibility
- **English:** Accessibility
- **Arabic:** إمكانية الوصول
- **Definition:** The design of products, devices, services, or environments to be usable by people with disabilities, including physical, sensory, and cognitive access.

### 6. Universal Design for Learning (UDL)
- **English:** Universal Design for Learning (UDL)
- **Arabic:** التصميم العالمي للتعلم
- **Definition:** An educational framework that offers flexibility in the ways information is presented, students respond, and engagement is fostered to reduce barriers in instruction.

### 7. Learning Disability
- **English:** Learning Disability
- **Arabic:** صعوبة تعلم
- **Definition:** A neurological disorder that affects the brain's ability to receive, process, store, and respond to information, impacting reading, writing, math, or reasoning skills.

### 8. Sensory Impairment
- **English:** Sensory Impairment
- **Arabic:** إعاقة حسية
- **Definition:** A disability that affects one or more senses, such as vision or hearing, requiring specific adaptations for learning.

### 9. Mainstreaming
- **English:** Mainstreaming
- **Arabic:** الدمج في التعليم العام
- **Definition:** The practice of integrating students with disabilities into general education classrooms for part or all of the school day, with appropriate support.

### 10. Reasonable Accommodation
- **English:** Reasonable Accommodation
- **Arabic:** تسهيلات معقولة
- **Definition:** Modifications or adjustments made to a learning environment, curriculum, or assessment that enable a student with a disability to participate equally.

## Notes
- Arabic translations are based on common usage in Palestinian educational contexts.
- Definitions are simplified for clarity and may be expanded in specific policy documents.
